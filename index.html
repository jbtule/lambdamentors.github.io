Lambda Mentors is a program that generates charitable donations by incentivizing the mentoring of people trying to enter the field of professional software development. Lambda Mentors is part of the Lambda Lounge, a tech user group in St. Louis, MO, focused on dynamic and functional programming languages and paradigms. Lambda Mentors works with Launch Code and the Coder Girl initiative to find learners who are ready to take the next step in pursuit of a career in software development.
